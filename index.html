FAQ

Is it okay to pet kittens I see outside?

Usually a brief, gentle pet is fine if the cat approaches you. Avoid handling tiny kittens.

Can I bring a kitten home?

Adoption and travel rules vary. Check official guidance from your airline and local authorities first.

What if I see a kitten in distress?

If safe to do so, note the location and contact a nearby veterinary clinic for advice.
